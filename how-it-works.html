Your Path to Wellness

A clear and supportive process, every step of the way.

Step 1

Meet Your Care Team

Your first visit is a 90-minute deep dive into your health history, lifestyle, and goals. We listen to your story to understand the complete picture of your health.

  • Comprehensive history review
  • Discuss your health goals
  • Initial nutrition & lifestyle assessment
Step 2

Get Smart Diagnostics

We use advanced testing to look "under the hood" and find data-driven answers. This goes beyond standard blood work to check for gut health, hormone levels, nutrient deficiencies, and more.

  • Advanced hormone panels
  • Gut microbiome analysis
  • Food sensitivity testing
Step 3

Receive Your Personal Plan

Your doctor and health coach build a 100% personalized plan based on your test results and goals. This is your roadmap to better health, and we support you the entire way.

  • Personalized nutrition plan
  • Supplement & medication protocol
  • Stress management & sleep strategies
  • Ongoing coaching & support
